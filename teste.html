SERN

SERN (Société Européenne de Recherche Nucléaire,
the European Society for Nuclear Research) is a global research organization secretly controlled by the Committee of 300,
the central antagonistic organization of the Science Adventure Series.
They are the main antagonist faction in Steins;Gate and a recurring minor faction in Steins;Gate 0.

John Titor

John Titor is an alias used by a person that claims to be a time traveler from the year 2036.
He appears at different times depending on the Attractor Field, appearing in the year 2010 in the Alpha Attractor Field,
and the year 2000 in the Beta Attractor Field.
